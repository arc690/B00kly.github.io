10. Binya's Blue Umbrella

Binya's Blue Umbrella is a fabulous book written by the famous author Ruskin Bond but is an underrated book and not much known, If you are looking for a book with a thrilling storyline then Binya's blue Umbrella is the best choice for you. In the story a mountain girl, who chances upon a blue umbrella and gets it for herself in exchange for her lucky charm. It gives you a lot of Entertainment and fun. It got 4.7/5 stars on Amazon.

9. Troubled Blood

Troubled Blood written by the famous author J.k Rowling but unlike Harry Potter, it couldn't gain the type of attention of the readers that Harry Potter took it got a 4/5 rating from the critics and was loved by the readers who read i. In the story, Private Detective Cormoran Strike is visiting his family in Cornwall when he was approached by a woman asking for help finding her mother, Margot Bamborough who went missing in mysterious circumstances in 1974. Strike has never tackled a cold case before, let alone one forty years old.

7. Lichgates

Lichgates is a very fascinating book by the author S. M. Boyce which gives us a deep message that a single choice can change our life. In the book we come to know about Kara Magari who uncovers an ancient book of dark and deadly magic, she realizes she's the only one who can read its enchanted pages... and overnight, she becomes the most coveted magical creature in not just one, but two worlds. ... In amazon, it got ratings of 4.3/5 which is brilliant. You can read this book if you are fond of magic!

6. Swami and Friends

Swami and Friends is a mixture of Comedy, suspense, and Fiction written by the Famous author RK Narayan. Swami is considered average among his friends, neither especially clever nor stupid, brave nor cowardly. It would be a fun novel to read with your friends and family! It got ratings of 4.6/5 on Amazon.

5. David copperfield

David Copperfield by Charles Dickens sold 25,000 copies at the start but later on it became the author's most famous book Unfortunately the book is not much popular among the young generation. The story is narrated by the eponymous David Copperfield, detailing his adventures in his journey from infancy to maturity. In amazon, it got a rating of 4.3/5.

4. Cujo

Cujo by Stephen King is a Horror Novel written in 1981. One day, Cujo chases a rabbit into a cave inhabited by sick bats and emerges as something new alltogether. Meanwhile, Vic and Donna Trenton, and their young son Tad, move to Maine. The novel won the British Fantasy Award in 1982 and then a film was also made following the story. It got a rating of 4.5/5 and is a 'MUST READ' book if you are fond of the horror genre!

3. The Hidden Hindu

The Hidden Hindu is based on Hindu mythology and is divided into a trilogy. In the story, Prithvi, a cryptic 21-year-old who remembers incidents from before his birth, is in pursuit of a mysterious middle-aged Aghori (Shiva devotee) who was tracked for more than 200 years before being captured and transported to a high-tech facility on an isolated island of India. It got a rating of 4.4/5 on Amazon. It is a must-read book if your fan of the fiction genre.

2. The Age of Wrath

The Age of Wrath is a history-based novel basically The best introduction to the labyrinth medieval India.The book narrates the history of the Delhi Sultans (rulers) who ruled over Delhi. If you have an interest in history then this book is a fabulous choice. It got a rating of 4.3/5 on Amazon. You can read this book in Free time!

1. The Trials of Appolo

'Last but not the least'The Trials of Appolo by Rick Riordan is a pentalogy of the genre of fantasy, adventure, and mythological fiction novels written by American author Rick Riordan that collectively form a sequel to the Heroes of Olympus series. This book is filled with entertaining jokes and humor and you will find it very interesting. It got the rating of 4.8/5 on Amazon!
